Computes canonical embedding. Requires p==-1 and m==2^k where k >=2 and f.length() < m/2. Sets v[m/4-1-i] = DFT[palg.ith_rep(i)] for i in range(m/4), where DFT[j] = f(W^j) for j in range(m), and W = exp(-2*pi*I/m).
